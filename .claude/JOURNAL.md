5. **Task [Extended] - mmBERT INT8 SmoothQuant notebook** (v0.1.0): Built notebook `00-kj-mmbert-int8-smoothquant.ipynb` quantizing any mmBERT checkpoint to INT8 with SmoothQuant and measuring quality<br>
   **Result**: Stage-0 model-prep notebook for the statement encoder. Post-training-quantizes `jhu-clsp/mmBERT-base` (multilingual ModernBERT, 307M params - the large vocab embedding dominates the 149M backbone) to W8A8 INT8, measuring how far INT8 perturbs the embedding geometry. Implemented SmoothQuant inline: forward-pre hooks collect per-input-channel activation maxima on a disjoint calibration corpus, the smoothing scale `s = act^alpha / weight^(1-alpha)` (alpha 0.5) folds into the preceding `LayerNorm` (divide) and the `Linear` input columns (multiply) - identity in FP, but it flattens the activation outliers that wreck naive INT8. Per the user's follow-up, generalized to any mmBERT (original or fine-tuned, Hub id or local path) via `AutoModel` encoder extraction, a `model_type=="modernbert"` guard, FP32 load, and try/except folds.
